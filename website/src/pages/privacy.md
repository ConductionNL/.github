# Privacy

Conduction's privacy and data protection practices are documented in two places:

- **Internal handbook (this site).** See [Privacy & AVG](/iso/privacy-policy/) under *Support & Safety* for the AVG/GDPR-aligned policy that applies to all Conduction projects.
- **Public-facing privacy statement.** Visit [conduction.nl](https://www.conduction.nl/) for the customer-facing privacy statement.

For privacy-related questions or DSAR requests, mail [info@conduction.nl](mailto:info@conduction.nl).
