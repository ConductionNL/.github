# Conduction Application Roadmap

> **Purpose**: This document outlines the current Conduction app portfolio and proposed gap apps based on market intelligence from 39,600 tenders across 8 countries. We are collecting colleague feedback on naming for the 10 planned applications.
>
> **How to give feedback**: Comment on the discussion thread with your preferred names or suggest alternatives. We want names that are short (5-12 characters), hint at functionality, and work in both Dutch and English — following the pattern of existing apps like Procest, Pipelinq, and Docudesk.

## Proposed Gap Apps

The following 10 applications address gaps identified through analysis of 7,662 relevant tenders and 97,819 requirements. Each app extends the Nextcloud-based municipal platform.

---

### 1. Sociaal Domein — Municipal Social Domain

**What it does**: Client intake, care assessment (WMO, Jeugdwet, Participatiewet), service tracking, care plans, provider management.

**Market evidence**: 162 tenders mention social domain terms across multiple categories (zaaksysteem, inkoop, erp, participatie). Social domain software is typically embedded in case management or ERP tenders rather than tendered as a standalone category, indicating strong cross-cutting demand.

**Name suggestions**:

| # | Name | Rationale |
|---|------|-----------|
| 1 | **Wijkdesk** | "Wijk" (neighborhood) + desk — social care is neighborhood-level |
| 2 | **Zorgregie** | "Zorg" (care) + "regie" (coordination) — care orchestration |
| 3 | **Zorgwijz** | "Zorg" (care) + "wijs" (wise/way) — smart care routing |
| 4 | **Sociaalq** | "Sociaal" + q suffix (Conduction pattern) |
| 5 | **Wijkzorg** | Neighborhood care — direct and clear |
| 6 | **Casozorg** | "Caso" (case, Esperanto) + zorg — case-based care |

| 10 | **Sociadesk** | "Sociaal" shortened + desk |

## How to Give Feedback

We want your input on naming these apps. For each domain:

1. **Pick your favorite** from the 10 suggestions, or
2. **Suggest a new name** that follows our naming pattern

Good names should:
- Be **5-12 characters** long
- **Hint at the functionality** (even if you have never seen the app)
- **Work in both Dutch and English** (or at least be pronounceable in both)
- Follow Conduction patterns: compound words, q-suffix, or -desk suffix

Please comment below with your preferences. We will finalize names before creating the repositories.
